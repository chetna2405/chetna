Is Your Food Pure?

Test Center

Learn simple methods to test common food items for purity.

Milk

Drop test: If milk spreads rapidly in water, it may be diluted or contain detergent.

Turmeric

Shake with water: A yellow layer floating on top may indicate lead chromate.

Honey

Flame test: Dip a cotton wick in honey and light it. Pure honey will burn cleanly.
